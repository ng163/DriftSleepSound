# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** May 22, 2026

Bin Hua ("we," "our," or "us") operates the **Drift Sleep Sound** mobile application (the "App") for iOS. 

Your privacy is highly important to us. This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of information when you use our App.

---

## 1. Information Collection and Use

### Personal Data
**Drift Sleep Sound does not collect, store, or transmit any of your personal data.** 
* We do not require you to create an account or provide any personal information (such as your name, email address, or phone number) to use the App.
* All features of the App, including playing white noise, setting timers, and mixing sounds, are designed to run locally on your device.

### Local Storage
The App may save certain configuration preferences locally on your iOS device using Apple's standard storage mechanisms (such as `UserDefaults`). This includes:
* Your premium version unlock status.
* Your last used custom timer settings (for Premium users).
* Your custom volume preferences.

This data remains strictly on your device and is never uploaded to any external servers.

---

## 2. In-App Purchases and Billing

If you purchase the premium upgrade via In-App Purchase:
* Transactions are processed securely and directly by Apple Inc. via the App Store (StoreKit).
* We do not collect, process, or have access to your payment information, credit card details, or billing address. 
* To restore your purchase on other iOS devices, the App may query Apple's App Store servers to verify your receipt. This verification is handled locally or directly through Apple's secure infrastructure.

---

## 3. Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate third-party analytics, tracking SDKs, or advertising networks. 

However, since the App is downloaded from and operates on Apple's iOS platform, your use of the App may be subject to Apple's standard Privacy Policy and terms regarding device diagnostics and system-level crash reporting (if you have opted-in to share these with developers).

---

## 4. Children’s Privacy

Because our App does not collect any personal information, it is safe for users of all ages, including children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly solicit or collect data from children.
